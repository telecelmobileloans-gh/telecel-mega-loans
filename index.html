FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Telecel Flex Loans.

What is Telecel Flex Loan?
+
Flex Loan is a mobile loan exclusively on Telecel Cash that allows eligible subscribers to borrow money directly into their Telecel Cash wallet — instantly, 24/7, without visiting a branch or providing paper documents. It is only for Telecel Cash wallet holders.
Who is eligible to apply?
+
You must be 18 years or older with an active Telecel Cash wallet. This service is exclusively for Telecel Cash users — regular Telecel subscribers without a Telecel Cash wallet are not eligible. Active, frequent Telecel Cash users (those who regularly send money, buy airtime, pay bills, and transact) enjoy higher eligibility and bigger loan limits.
Who funds the Flex Loan?
+
The loan is funded by First Atlantic Bank (FAB), a fully licensed financial institution in Ghana. Telecel Ghana facilitates access through the Telecel Cash mobile platform on behalf of FAB.
How does being a regular Telecel user affect my limit?
+
Your Telecel Cash wallet activity directly determines your loan eligibility. The more you transact — sending money, paying bills, buying airtime, receiving payments — the higher your assessed limit becomes. Regular, consistent use of Telecel Cash signals financial activity and trustworthiness, unlocking access to progressively larger loan amounts over time.
How do I apply for a Flex Loan?
+
Apply through this portal by entering your Telecel Cash number, or via the USSD loan menu on your phone. The entire process takes under 5 minutes with no documents required. Simply verify your number, choose your amount and duration, review the summary, and accept.
What fees are charged?
+
A service fee of 8.9% per month applies, prorated for the exact duration you choose (7 days, 14 days, 21 days, 1 month, 2 months, up to 6 months). If not repaid by the due date, a one-time late payment penalty of 12.5% is added to the outstanding balance. No other hidden fees apply.
What loan durations are available?
+
You can choose from: 7 days, 14 days, 21 days, 1 month (30 days), 2 months, 3 months, 4 months, 5 months, or 6 months. The service fee is calculated proportionally based on the duration you select, so a shorter period means a lower total fee.
What will I see before accepting the loan?
+
Before accepting, you clearly see: loan amount, duration, service fee, total repayment amount, due date, and any applicable late fees. You must explicitly tap "Accept" before any funds are disbursed. Nothing is taken without your consent.
Can I have more than one active loan?
+
No. You cannot access another Flex Loan while you have an existing outstanding loan. Once your current loan is fully repaid, you can immediately apply for a new one — and regular repayment history helps grow your limit.
How do I repay the loan?
+
You can repay by: (1) allowing automatic deduction from your Telecel Cash wallet on the due date, or (2) manually repaying — partially or in full — via the repayment option in your Telecel Cash menu at any time before the due date. Early repayment is always welcome with no penalty.
